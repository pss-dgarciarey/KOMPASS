# Community Policy

Kompass is publicly visible and source-available, but it is maintained under
the direction of the project owner.

This is a small, opinionated project. The goal is useful signal, not endless
process theatre.

## Expected behavior

- Stay respectful and technically grounded.
- Focus criticism on claims, methods, data, and code.
- Do not harass, intimidate, or spam.
- Do not publish private information without consent.

## Moderation

The project owner may remove discussions, reject submissions, or deny access to
any interaction that is abusive, disruptive, misleading, or inconsistent with
the project's licensing and operating rules.

If something has to be moderated, the simplest workable intervention wins.
